FedaPay offers you a simple and fast solution to manage online payments. Whether you are an entrepreneur, an NGO, or a freelancer, FedaPay simplifies payment collection while ensuring the security of your transactions. Follow this guide to create and activate your account, discover FedaPay use cases, and enhance your account security.

### Create a FedaPay Account (Test or Live)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Go to [FedaPay's website](https://www.fedapay.com/?v=ddd70bbaf5be) and click on "**Sign Up**".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Form">
    Enter your basic information such as your name and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Confirmation">
    Check your inbox for the FedaPay confirmation email and click on the validation link, whether you are in test (sandbox) mode or live mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Mode">
    After signing up, you can immediately start exploring all features in Test mode without real charges.

    ![Sandbox account sign-up form](https://res.cloudinary.com/dvilp6td2/image/upload/v1730990802/img_form_sandbox-compte_z55un5.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Account Validation (Live Accounts Only)
To accept real payments, you need to activate your Live account. Here’s how:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Your Account for Validation">
    Log in to your dashboard, go to settings, and click on "**Activate My Account**".

    ![Live account activation](https://res.cloudinary.com/dvilp6td2/image/upload/v1730991613/img_active_live-compte_y8yh3h.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the Form">
    Enter your organization name, business sector, and upload the required documents.

    <Note>
    **Verification Documents (KYC)** 

    Depending on your account type, certain information is required:

      - **NGO**: This account is intended for non-governmental organizations. No transaction amount limits.

      _**Required Documents**: Association creation document, ID card._
      
      - **Freelancer**: Designed for freelancers, this account allows a maximum of 10 transactions per week, with a limit of 100 XOF to 300,000 XOF per transaction.

      _**Required Documents**: ID card, Tax ID (IFU), signature._

      - **Business**: For formally registered businesses, with no transaction amount limits.

      _**Required Documents**: RCCM, Tax ID (IFU), ID card, signature._

      - **Government**: Account intended for governmental institutions, with no transaction amount limits.
    </Note>

### FedaPay Use Cases

The following use cases illustrate how you can use FedaPay to collect and payout payments based on your business needs:

#### Collects

_**Scenario: Quickly Receive Payments with a Payment Link**_

If you need to receive money from clients without complex technical integration, FedaPay offers a simple and effective solution. In just a few minutes, you can generate a secure payment link to send directly to your clients.

**Steps to Receive a Payment with a Payment Link**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Customer">
    In your FedaPay dashboard, add a new customer by filling in basic information: first name, last name, and email address (the email is unique and essential for customer tracking). The phone number is optional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a collect">
    A transaction represents the amount you expect from the customer. Enter the transaction amount, add a description (mandatory), and, if needed, customize the return link after payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a Payment Link">
    Once the transaction is created, a secure payment link is automatically generated. You can send it to the customer via email, WhatsApp, SMS, or any other communication channel. The link is valid for 24 hours and ensures a secure transaction processed by FedaPay.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Transaction Statuses**:
  - **pending**: Waiting (default status upon creation)
  - **approved**: Approved (successful payment)
  - **declined**: Declined (insufficient funds or payment issue)
  - **canceled**: Canceled (voluntary or accidental interruption by the customer)
  - **refunded**: Refunded (amount returned to the customer)
  - **transferred**: Transferred (amount sent to the merchant account)
</Note>

#### Payouts

_**Scenario: How an Online Betting Company Makes Payouts to a Client?**_

In the case of winnings payouts, online betting companies can use FedaPay to facilitate deposits to their clients. This process simplifies payment delivery, offering clients a secure and fast experience.

**Steps to Make a Payout to a Client**:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Payout">
    In the FedaPay dashboard, the betting company creates a payout for the winning client, specifying the winning amount and the client’s destination account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Validate and Start the Payout">
    Once the payout is created, its initial status is **pending** (waiting). After validating the payout request, the status changes to **started** (started), indicating the transfer preparation.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Processing and Sending">
    FedaPay begins processing the payout. At this stage, the status changes to **processing** (in progress), indicating that the transfer is being finalized.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Completion">
    Once the payout is completed, the status changes to **sent** (sent), confirming that the client has received the payment.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Failure Management">
    If the payout fails (technical error or issue related to the payout method), the status changes to **failed**. In this case, resolution actions can be taken to complete the payment transfer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Payout Statuses**:
- **pending**: Waiting (initial status after payout creation)
- **started**: Started (the payout has been validated)
- **processing**: In Progress (payout being processed)
- **sent**: Sent (payout completed successfully)
- **failed**: Failed (payout was not successful due to an error)
</Note>

### Security: Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Security is a priority at FedaPay. That’s why we offer Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to protect your account and transactions. Enabling 2FA adds an extra layer of security by requiring a unique authentication code in addition to your password.

**How to Enable 2FA**:
- Go to your dashboard and navigate to security settings.
  Click on **Enable 2FA**.
- Follow the instructions to link your account to an authenticator app like Google Authenticator.
- Each time you log in, you will need to enter a unique code generated by your 2FA app.

_**Security Tip**: Use a strong and unique password for your FedaPay account and avoid sharing it._
