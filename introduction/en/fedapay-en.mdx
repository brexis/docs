##### ***Welcome to FedaPay: Simplify your Online Payments!***

### Overview

**FedaPay** is an innovative online payment platform designed to meet the needs of freelancers, businesses, e-commerce platforms, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and government institutions. With its intuitive interface and quick integration, FedaPay not only facilitates online payment acceptance but also allows payouts to users' Mobile Money accounts. The platform ensures efficient and secure transaction management, as well as seamless financial flow tracking, making payment and payout operations simple and reliable.

### Key Features

#### Collects

FedaPay offers simple and secure online payment solutions that make collecting funds easy and efficient with your clients.

* **Payment Link** : Create and share a unique payment link that lets your clients pay easily without complex integration.

* **Payment Page** : Set up a dedicated payment page that can be customized with your brand colors.

* **FedaPay Integration** : Add flexible and straightforward payment solutions to your website using **libraries** available for various languages and frameworks, or choose integration via the [FedaPay Checkout.js widget](/libraries/en/checkoutjs-en). This solution allows you to choose between direct integration, for full control and advanced customization, or integration with redirection, which directs your clients to a fast and user-friendly payment interface. Perfect for developers seeking a complete experience adaptable to their site’s needs.

#### Payouts

FedaPay’s payout feature enables direct fund transfers to Mobile Money accounts, providing easy access to liquidity, particularly useful for users in Africa where Mobile Money is widely used.

<Note>**Note**: Payouts are currently only possible for Mobile Money accounts, allowing users to easily receive funds collected via FedaPay directly on their mobile phones.
</Note>
